AWS STS

Checks two gates

  • Provider trusts GitHub's issuer
  • Role trust policy matches:
  •   aud == sts.amazonaws.com
  •   sub LIKE repo:...:*
No access keys. No secrets in GitHub. The role can push to llm-app and nothing else.
